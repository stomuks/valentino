ABOUT ME

I’m Valentina Drofa. My career began in finance in 2007, where I built a solid foundation of expertise and connections. This knowledge proved invaluable when I moved into EdTech and founded my first company.

In 2010, I launched an educational platform focused on trading and investing. It quickly grew to reach over 1 million students and hosted around 100 expert-led webinars every month.

A year later, responding to increasing demand from corporate clients, I founded Drofa Comms — a PR agency serving finance and fintech companies worldwide.

From traditional banks, brokers and exchanges to innovative blockchain startups, our agency helps leading brands build credibility, grow influence, and navigate the complexity of financial markets.

At Drofa Comms, we operate based on four core values: integrity, respect, growth, proactivity. These principles shape how we work — both with each other and with our clients.

How I can help your business

Strategic advisory for founders & executives

I work directly with business leaders to refine strategy, shape products, and unlock new growth opportunities. My hands-on approach and operational experience help uncover blind spots and deliver actionable insights — especially in fintech and finance.

Crisis management & risk planning

We take a comprehensive approach to crisis management — from identifying risks early to preparing multiple response scenarios and full-scale action plans. It’s not just about communications; it’s about operational readiness and awareness. We help businesses develop in-house protocols and response teams to stay prepared long before a crisis hits.

Executive brand strategy

Through individual consulting and team-led implementation, I help founders and CEOs develop strong personal brands. Becoming known — not just successful — increases visibility with investors, customers, and partners. Having a personal brand also shapes the face of your company and helps it grow.

International projects

With extensive global experience, Drofa Comms’ team designs and leads high-impact international initiatives that define market leadership and build industry authority. These campaigns help brands secure their niche and gain recognition on a global stage.

Speaking & media training

As a regular speaker at global conferences on PR, business growth, and fintech, I share insights that resonate with modern audiences. I also offer private media training for company owners — helping you show up to events and interview with confidence, clear messages, and credibility.
